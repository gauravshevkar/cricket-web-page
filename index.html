About Cricket

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of eleven players on a field at the center of which is a 22-yard pitch with a wicket at each end, each comprising two bails balanced on three stumps.





FAQs

Cricket is a game played with a bat and ball between two teams of eleven players.

Cricket is primarily played in three formats: Test matches, One Day Internationals (ODIs), and T20s.

The captain is responsible for leading the team and making strategic decisions on the field.
